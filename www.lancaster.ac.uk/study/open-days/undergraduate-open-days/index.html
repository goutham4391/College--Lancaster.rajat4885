Undergraduate Open Days

Our undergraduate open days are the best way for you to get a feel for us.

Undergraduate Digital Open Event - Three Days of Lancaster

Wednesday 26 - Friday 28 October 2022

Over the course of three days, we’ll be running a range of online sessions to show you all that Lancaster has to offer.

Join us for just one hour or for the full three days, it’s completely up to you!

Book our UG digital event

What to expect from an open day

Seeing is believing and we think the best way to get to know Lancaster is to join us online or in-person. But, what can you expect to hear about?

A tour of the Campus

Join Meenal and Vlad as they take you on a tour of the Lancaster University campus. Discover the learning facilities, accommodation, sports facilities, welfare, cafes, bars, parkland and more.

Meet us

Come and discover what makes Lancaster so special. With a range of on-campus, in-person or online events, we would love to meet you.

  • Open days and campus tours

    See our beautiful campus and facilities. Chat to staff about your degree or find out what it's really like to study at Lancaster from our students.

  • Connect with us online

    If you can't visit us in person there are still lots of ways you can connect with Lancaster University and find out about life on campus.
