Postgraduate entry qualifications

The information provided below is relevant to 2022 entry and may change for future entry years.

The entry requirements for our postgraduate taught degrees can be found on the individual postgraduate course webpages. Our entry requirements are listed using the standard UK undergraduate degree classifications (eg First Class honours or 2:1 for an Upper Second).
