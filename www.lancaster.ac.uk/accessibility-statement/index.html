Accessibility Statements

Information about the accessibility of our websites and online services

Making our websites and mobile applications accessible to students, staff, potential applicants and other users is part of our aim to improve equality of opportunity and remove barriers for groups and individuals to allow them to flourish in their research, teaching, studies, engagement, administration, service and life at the University.

These pages set out how we work to make our websites and online services compliant in accordance with The Public Sector Bodies (Websites and Mobile Applications) Accessibility Regulations 2018 No.952.

You can find out more about our vision for Equality, Diversity and Inclusion (EDI) on the Lancaster University EDI web pages.
