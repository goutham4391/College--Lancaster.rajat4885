Why Lancaster?

For us, Lancaster is everything a university should be. It's somewhere you can belong, a community to join with a collection of colleges that offer close-knit support and proud identity. We set high standards and challenges, pushing people to new heights. We support and guide people through their studies, research and careers.

Lancaster ranked 1st in North and 8th in England for student satisfaction

Lancaster University has been given an overall satisfaction score of 83.6% in this year’s National Student Survey (NSS), ranking 1st in the North, 8th in England and 11th in the UK when compared to other universities.

Positive change: carbon net-zero by 2035

Sustainability is at the heart of Lancaster University. We declared a climate emergency in 2020 and aim to become carbon neutral by 2035. Discover more about our sustainability strategy, our world-leading research and our achievements so far.

Global Lancaster

Lancaster University has partnerships all around the world, including four strategic teaching partnerships in China, Germany, Ghana and Malaysia. These enhance the global experience available to our students, enrich our curriculum and create opportunities for collaboration on world-leading, transformative research.
